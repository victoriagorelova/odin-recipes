Broccoli Chicken Casserole

Description

When you're in need of a quick and easy comforting dinner, look no further than cheesy chicken broccoli stuffing casserole.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Broccoli
  • Stuffing Mix
  • Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • Mayonnaise
  • Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. Place chicken breasts in a large pot; add enough water to completely cover chicken. Bring to a gentle simmer; cover tightly with a lid and cook until chicken is no longer pink in centers, about 15 minutes.
  4. Transfer chicken to a cutting board. Cut into bite-sized pieces when cool enough to handle.
  5. Combine condensed soup and mayonnaise in a bowl.
  6. Place chicken into a 9x13-inch baking dish; layer cooked broccoli, soup mixture, and Cheddar cheese over top.
  7. Sprinkle dry stuffing mix over top.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly and cheese has melted, 25 to 30 minutes.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!
